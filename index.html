My Name is Michael

Hi! My name is Michael, and I am 12 years old. I come from Lagos State, Nigeria, and I live with my family. I have two sisters, and we all enjoy spending time together. Sometimes we play games, watch movies, or help each other with schoolwork.

I am currently in Junior Secondary School, and my favorite subjects are Mathematics, Basic Science, and Computer Studies. I really enjoy learning how things work, especially technology and computers. One day, I hope to become a Tech Engineer. I want to design and build amazing things using computers, machines, and maybe even robots!

I am very curious and always love to ask questions and explore new things. I enjoy using the internet to learn, especially watching videos that teach about programming, how websites work, or how to build apps.

Apart from school, I also enjoy playing football, reading interesting books, and sometimes helping my parents around the house. I try my best to be kind, respectful, and hardworking because I believe that good character is just as important as being smart.

In the future, I hope to study Engineering or Computer Science at a good university. I want to use my knowledge to solve real problems in my community and help others through technology.

That’s a little about me, and I’m excited for all the things I’ll learn and do as I grow!
